Smash Potatoes

Ingredients: 6 small potatoes (any kind, we’re not picky) A splash of olive oil (or butter if you're feeling fancy) Salt, pepper, and something green (parsley?) for flair

Instructions: Boil those bad boys until they’re soft enough to smash (fork test works). Lay them on a baking sheet and smash with the bottom of a cup. (Channel your rage here!) Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and bake at 425°F until crispy. Garnish with parsley if you remembered to buy it. Serve immediately to avoid sogginess.
