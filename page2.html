Fried Chix Sammy

Ingredients: 1 Fried chicken breast (any seasoning works!) A bun A handful of lettuce (or whatever green stuff is lying around) 2 slices of tomato Lots of Mayo

Instructions: 1. Slap mayo on both sides of the bun 2. Place the chicken breast on one slice, top with lettuce and tomato. 3. Crown it with the other slice of bread. 4. Smash it slightly for dramatic effect and eat immediately (preferably standing up).
